Sampling (Spatial Resolution)

Digitizing coordinate values. It determines the number of pixels representing an image. Lower sampling rate leads to a pixelated pattern (checkerboard effect).

Quantization (Intensity Resolution)

Digitizing amplitude values. It determines the number of colors or gray levels available. Lower quantization leads to false contouring (color banding).
